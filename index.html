Link A Job

Founder of 'Link A Job', - A revolutionary Career site made with the collaboration of Government of Nepal's Ministry of Social Development.

Founder of 'Link A Job', - A revolutionary Career site made with the collaboration of Government of Nepal's Ministry of Social Development (Lumbini Province Youth Council) and various private organizations. 'Link A Job' is dedicated to transforming the job search experience in Nepal by eliminating commission fees and registration charges for jobseekers. Our platform ensures that jobseekers receive fair pay and a hassle-free experience, making it easier for them to find meaningful employment opportunities. We strive to create a better job market in Nepal by bridging the gap between employers and jobseekers, fostering a transparent and efficient job search environment.
